Available BHS-Offered Sites

The Student Hub offers a variety of formal and informal sites to support students in their academic journey. This number is constantly being updated and improved, so be sure to check back often for new resources! All credit to their respective developers.

BHS Official Site

Barcelona high School's official website, offering additional information and admissions details.

Veracross

Can use to view your (or your student's) grades, attendance, and other academic information.

Accelerate Platform

Shows course content, assignments, and your to-do list. Some classes may not be present.

Google Classroom

Shows class announcements and study resources for each class. Some teachers may post assignments and reminders.

BHS Bell

Shows period schedule and calendar information for BHS students.

BHS Newspaper

Shows current as well as archived BHS newsletters, newspapers, and magazines.

BHS Merch Store

Buy BHS hoodies, shirts, accessories, and other merchandise here.
