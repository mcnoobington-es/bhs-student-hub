BHS Code of Conduct

Barcelona High School (BHS) community is founded on principles of respect, equity, and integrity. The Code of Conduct serves as a guide for all students, staff, and stakeholders to ensure a positive, safe, and respectful learning environment. All members of the BHS community are expected to adhere to this code and uphold our shared values.

Code of Conduct

Effective date: August 1, 2025
Last updated: August 1, 2025

Respect and Civility
Treat all members of the BHS community with respect, courtesy, and dignity, regardless of their background, identity, or differences.

Conflict Resolution
Resolve conflicts peacefully and constructively. Avoid engaging in disputes, bullying, or any form of harassment.

Academic Integrity
Maintain academic honesty and integrity in all coursework, assessments, and assignments. Submit your own work, ideas, and research, properly citing sources when necessary.

Safety and Well-Being
Ensure the physical safety and well-being of oneself and others, refraining from any form of harm or endangerment. Be mindful of the emotional and mental well-being of your peers and seek assistance or support when needed.

School Property and Resources
Care for and respect school property, facilities, and resources. Report any damage or misuse. Use technology and digital resources responsibly and in accordance with BHS policies.

Inclusivity and Diversity
Promote an inclusive and diverse environment by respecting and embracing the backgrounds, cultures, and identities of others.

Attendance and Punctuality
Attend school regularly and punctually. Notify the school in advance when you must be absent.

Alcohol, Drugs, and Contraband
Do not possess, use, distribute, or be under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any contraband on school premises, during the school day, representing the school in any capacity and at any school functions.

Consequences
Failure to comply with this Code of Conduct may result in consequences, which could include verbal or written warnings, parental involvement, disciplinary actions, and possible suspension or expulsion.

Reporting
If you are aware of any violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to a trusted staff member or administrator.
