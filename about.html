About Student Hub

Student Hub is a platform designed to help students manage their academic life more effectively. Our mission is to provide tools and resources that enhance the student experience.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2025
Last updated: November 17, 2025

Student Hub (Hereto referred as “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website [studenthub.com] (the “Site”).

1. Information We Collect
Our Site is designed to minimize the collection of personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personal information from visitors.
The only interaction that may involve personal information is when a user chooses to contact us via the “Contact” button, which will open the user’s email application. We do not store or process any information submitted through email.

2. Cookies and Tracking
Our Site does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics tools.

3. Third-Party Links
Our Site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any linked sites.

4. Data Sharing and Storage
We do not share, sell, or store any user data. Any emails sent via the contact button are handled solely by your email provider and are not stored on our Site.

5. Children's Privacy
Our Site is intended for use by students, including minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise their children’s online activities.

6. Your Rights
Since we do not collect or store personal information, there are no personal data rights to exercise on the Site itself.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.

8. Contact Us
For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at msmith7297@gmail.com or via the contact button above.
